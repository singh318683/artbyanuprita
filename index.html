✦ The Artist

Anuprita Khare

Over the last six years, I've had the incredible privilege of learning directly from traditional master folk painters across India. This journey has allowed me to explore and practice 81 different folk art styles — from celebrating well-known Indian traditions like Pattachitra, Cheriyal, and Kalamkari, to preserving rare, unique forms like Kurumba and Kavad.

To keep these ancient traditions as authentic as possible, I steer clear of store-bought materials. Instead, I make my own paints from natural pigments, and use handmade papers and traditional canvases only.

✦ Custom Artwork

Commission a Painting

Request an original piece in any of the 81 folk art styles — for your home, a gift, a wedding, or a heritage project. Every commission is made entirely with natural pigments.

How it works

  1. Share your vision — the style, subject, size, and occasion. The more detail you give, the better the result.
  2. Anuprita reviews your request and replies with a timeline and pricing estimate within 3–5 days.
  3. A 50% advance confirms the commission. Work begins; only natural pigments and handmade materials are used.
  4. Progress photos are shared at key milestones. Final delivery with a signed certificate of authenticity.

Pricing guide

Small (A4) · ₹4,000 – ₹8,000
Medium (A3) · ₹8,000 – ₹18,000
Large (A2+) · ₹18,000 and above
Prices vary with style complexity and materials.

Delivery

Paintings are shipped carefully rolled in archival tissue. Within India: 7–10 days after completion. International: 14–21 days via tracked courier.
