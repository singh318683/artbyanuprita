Copyright & Usage Policy

Every painting on this site represents months of learning, natural materials, and living tradition. Here's how that work is protected — and how you're welcome to enjoy and share it.

1 Ownership

All original paintings, photographs, written content, and artwork descriptions on this website are the sole property of Anuprita Khare and are protected under applicable copyright law. Anuprita retains full copyright and intellectual property rights over every piece shown here, unless a specific work has been sold or licensed in writing — in which case only the terms of that specific sale or license apply, and copyright does not automatically transfer with a physical painting unless explicitly stated.

2 What You May Do
  • View and enjoy the artwork on this site for personal, non-commercial purposes.
  • Share a direct link to this website or to a specific painting's page.
  • Share an image on social media, provided it is shared in full — uncropped and unedited — and clearly credited to "Anuprita Khare" or "@artbyanuprita" with a link back to this site.
3 What You May Not Do
  • Reproduce, copy, or download artwork images for redistribution.
  • Sell, print, or manufacture products (cards, canvases, merchandise, etc.) using these images.
  • Use any artwork for commercial purposes, including advertising, packaging, or promotional material.
  • Claim any artwork, in whole or in part, as your own original work.
  • Crop, edit, filter, or remove any watermark, signature, or attribution from an image.
4 Licensing & Permission Requests

Interested in using a piece for press coverage, an educational resource, a curated exhibition, or another specific purpose? Licensing and permission are considered on a case-by-case basis. Please reach out with:

  • The specific painting(s) or content you'd like to use
  • Where and how it will be used (publication, classroom, exhibition, etc.)
  • The intended audience and duration of use
5 Reporting a Violation or Requesting Use

If you'd like to request permission for a specific use, or if you've spotted unauthorized use of any artwork from this site elsewhere, please get in touch directly:

Email: artbyanupritaa@gmail.com

Please include links, screenshots, or details of the artwork in question so it can be addressed as quickly as possible.
